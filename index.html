About Trigger 2K22

National Level Technical Symposium

It’s time to Acknowledge who we are!

The Department of the Computer Science and Engineering, RajaRajeswari College of Engineering (RRCE) loves to help students augment the theoretical knowledge gained by them with a strong practical approach and promote innovation. The members of CSE Association have planned to conduct a National Level Technical symposium “Trigger 2K22” on 31st May 2022. The main objective is to provide a platform for the students to interact and exchange their ideas.
